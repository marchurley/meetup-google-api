Find a Meetup in your City

Instructions

  • Enter a city name (worldwide) or zip code (US only)
  • Zoom in and out of the map as necessary
  • Hoover over markers to show group names
  • Click on markers to get more information about the group and a link to the groups meetup page

Information

  • Meetup only supports location accuracy by zip code within the USA, Canda and GB. All other countries will only show 1 marker per city. Just click on the marker and you will find a link to the complete list of listings in that city
  • If it can't find the city, the results will be based on your registered Meetup profile location, falling back on your IP's geographic location
